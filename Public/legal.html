Privacy Policy

HR Cottage is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect it.

Our web servers automatically collect routine information about your visit, including IP addresses, browser details, timestamps, and referring pages. This data is used solely for the purpose of site administration and maintenance. It does not personally identify individual visitors.

Cookies and Web Beacons


To enhance your experience, HR Cottage may use cookies to store your preferences and track your interactions with our site. This enables us to provide customized content and improve our services. Our advertising partners and third-party services may also use cookies, scripts, or web beacons to track visitors and serve targeted advertisements.

You have control over your privacy settings. Your browser allows you to disable cookies if you prefer not to have your data tracked.
